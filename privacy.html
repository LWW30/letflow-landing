Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 2026

LetFlowUK (“we”, “us”, “our”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights. LetFlowUK is currently in beta and operated by Luke Walker, Derby, United Kingdom.

1. What information we collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: your email address when you join the waitlist or create an account.
  • Property and tenancy data: property addresses, room details, tenant names, rent amounts, and payment records that you enter into the app.
  • Correspondence: emails or messages you send to us at hello@letflowuk.com.
  • Usage data: pages visited, features used, and general interaction with the app (collected anonymously via standard server logs).

We do not collect payment card details. Any future subscription billing will be handled securely by Stripe, a third-party payment processor.

2. How we use your information

  • To provide, maintain and improve the LetFlowUK service.
  • To send you product updates, launch notifications, and service emails (you can opt out at any time).
  • To respond to your support requests.
  • To comply with our legal obligations under UK law.

We will never sell your personal data to third parties or use it for advertising purposes.

3. Data storage and security

Your data is stored securely using Supabase, a UK/EU-compliant database platform hosted on AWS. Data is encrypted at rest and in transit. We use row-level security policies to ensure landlords can only access their own data.

We take reasonable technical and organisational measures to protect your data, but no system is 100% secure. If we become aware of a breach that affects your data, we will notify you as required by UK GDPR.

4. Cookies

LetFlowUK uses only essential cookies necessary for the service to function (e.g. authentication session tokens). We do not use tracking cookies or third-party advertising cookies.

5. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate LetFlowUK:

  • Supabase — database and authentication (GDPR-compliant)
  • Vercel — app hosting
  • Netlify — landing page hosting
  • Resend — transactional email delivery
  • Stripe — payment processing (when billing is activated)

Each of these providers has their own privacy policy and processes data on our behalf under data processing agreements.

6. Your rights under UK GDPR

As a UK resident, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct any inaccurate data.
  • Delete your data (“right to be forgotten”).
  • Restrict or object to how we process your data.
  • Data portability — receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • Withdraw consent at any time where processing is based on consent.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@letflowuk.com. We will respond within 30 days.

7. Data retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. If you close your account, we will delete your personal data within 30 days, except where we are required by law to retain it longer.

Waitlist email addresses are retained until you unsubscribe or LetFlowUK closes.

8. Children

LetFlowUK is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of any material changes by email. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will always reflect the most recent version.

10. Contact us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or how we handle your data, please contact us:

  • Email: hello@letflowuk.com
  • Website: letflowuk.com

If you are unhappy with our response, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Information Commissioner’s Office (ICO), the UK’s data protection authority.
