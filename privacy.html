Privacy Policy

Last updated: 21 April 2026

LetFlowUK is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains how we collect, use and store information when you use app.letflowuk.com.

1. Who we are

LetFlowUK is a UK-based HMO management platform. Questions: hello@letflowuk.com

2. Data we collect

  • Account data: email address and hashed password
  • Property data: addresses, rooms, HMO licence details you enter
  • Tenant data: names, contacts, tenancy and deposit information
  • Financial data: rent amounts, expenses, payment records
  • Usage data: pages visited and features used

3. How we use your data

  • To provide and improve LetFlowUK
  • To send transactional emails (rent reminders, maintenance alerts)
  • To process subscription payments via Stripe
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal basis

We process your data on the basis of contract (to deliver the service) and legitimate interests (improving the platform, fraud prevention).

5. Who we share data with

  • Supabase — database and authentication (EU data centres)
  • Stripe — payment processing
  • Resend — transactional email
  • Vercel — application hosting

We do not sell your data to any third party.

6. Retention

Data is retained while your account is active. On deletion, personal data is removed within 30 days unless retention is required by law.

7. Your rights (UK GDPR)

You have the right to access, correct, delete, or port your data. Email: hello@letflowuk.com

8. Cookies

We use only essential authentication cookies. No advertising or tracking cookies.

9. Security

All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS) and at rest. Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text.

10. Changes

We will notify you of significant changes by email.
