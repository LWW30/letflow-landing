Features

Everything a solo HMO landlord needs

💷
Rent tracking per room
See who has paid, who is due, who is overdue - per room, per property, at a glance. No more spreadsheets or bank statement checking.
📋
Compliance dashboard
Gas Safe, EICR, EPC, HMO licence - all your certificates tracked with expiry alerts. Avoid fines up to 30,000 per offence.
🔧
Maintenance tracking
Log issues, track progress, keep timestamps. Critical legal protection now Section 21 is abolished and tenants are harder to remove.
⚡
Shared bills split
Add gas, electric, WiFi and water - LetFlow splits them across tenants automatically. Bills-included HMOs made simple.
🔗
Tenant portal
Each tenant gets a private link to see their balance and submit maintenance requests. No app download, no login hassle.
📄
Document vault
Store tenancy agreements, inspection reports and certificates per property. Everything searchable in seconds when the council visits.
Why LetFlow

HMOs are not like normal rentals

Most landlord software was built for single-lets. HMOs have multiple tenants, individual rent schedules, bills to split, compliance headaches, and tougher eviction rules. LetFlow is built from scratch for house shares.

  • ✓Bills split across tenants automatically
  • ✓Simple enough to ditch the letting agent
